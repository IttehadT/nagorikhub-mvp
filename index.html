Our Motivation

Why we built this.

Every year, millions of Bangladeshis relocate for work, education, and family — often making one of their biggest financial decisions with almost no reliable data.

Existing rental platforms show you photos and a price. They don't tell you whether the gas lines work, how often the electricity cuts out, or whether the neighbourhood is safe for a tutor visiting at 9pm.

NagorikHub was born from that frustration. We believe that renting a home in Dhaka shouldn't be a leap of faith. It should be an informed decision backed by verified data, real sentiment, and AI that actually understands local context.

Nagorik means citizen in Bengali. This platform is built for citizens, by citizens — a community intelligence layer that gets smarter every day.
